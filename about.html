About us :


Aajkamuhurat is a heartfelt project from the house of two brothers trying to create things that are simple, not nonsense & effective.


In the world of Intagram trends, we also wish to understand our roots with utmost sincerity & share it with as many people as we can.
Muhurat is something that we never really understood fully but always followed as per the instructions of our daadi ma, naani ma & maa. You see no matter how successful or accomplished you are in life, you still are their little kids & there is this whole school of culture that we somehow slipped to understand fully due to the hyper competitive environment in our professional life & schooling life.


This website is an attempt to share the relevant logics behind muhurat, a very simple and minimalistic approach to keeping a track of muhurats & chogadiyas (including jain chogadiya) and to compile together all the bhajans & stories that bring us closer to our Indian roots.


Join us in creating honest & beautiful things.
We'd love to hear your recommendations about the website.

Take good care everyone!

I am Siddharth & I will keep you updated with the progress we make on this website on a regular & honest basis.
